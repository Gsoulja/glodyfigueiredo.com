• ENGENHARIA INFORMÁTICA – Escola Superior de Tecnologia e Gestão de Viseu

    15/09/2017 - NOW

    The Computer Engineering course favors a predominantly practical teaching model, contemplating the application of the knowledge acquired in laboratory practices and a curricular project/internship. The course aims to Train qualified specialists for the competent exercise of a professional activity, able to properly apply the Information and Communication Technologies (ICT), combining factors of technical, economic, organizational and social nature; To prepare future professionals to respond to the constant evolution of ICT and development methodologies, as well as to the challenges posed by the highly competitive and globalized business environment; To train professionals who combine a high technical level with a strong capacity for human relationships, so that graduates can easily fit into networked and collaborative work environments that are constantly changing. The Computer Engineering degree has the following professional outlets: Analysis, planning and development of information systems; Database administration and management; Integration of computer systems in heterogeneous environments; Planning, installation, configuration and management of complex communication systems and networks; Administration, management and maintenance of systems or computer centers; Consulting and auditing of Systems and Information Technology.
